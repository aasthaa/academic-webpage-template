Heagerty (Biomarker and Longitudinal Data) Working Group

I organize weekly meetings for PhD students and Postdoctoral Fellows working with Dr Patrick Heagerty to learn about and contribute to each other's research. Topics range from prognostic and treatment-selection biomarkers to rate regression methods, outcome dependent sampling and methods for longitudinal count data.

Time: Every Tuesday, 3:30pm
Location: UW Tower, 22nd floor
